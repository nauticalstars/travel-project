Why Whistler?

One of the largest ski resorts in North America

Whistler is a town north of Vancouver, British Columbia, that's home to Whistler Blackcomb, one of the largest ski resorts in North America. Besides skiing and snowboarding, the area offers snowshoeing, tobogganing and ski jumping at the Olympic Park, a venue for the 2010 Vancouver Winter Olympics. The hub of Whistler is a compact, chalet-style pedestrian village at the base of Whistler and Blackcomb mountains.

My favourite restaurants in Whistler

Cows Ice Cream

The ice cream is handmade and made of using the highest quality ingredients, including 16% butterfat. Our old-fashioned recipe is churned slowly, adding limited amounts of air so each bite of ice cream is creamy and packed with flavour! Every scoop contains fresh cream, real eggs, and is served in a handmade waffle cone.

Address

Unit 1 - 4313 Main Street

What I like about it

They have many different flavours available. They make the waffle cones daily, and they also sell other things like shirts and snacks. The milkshakes are also really good.

Harajuku whistler

Harajuku's interior is inspired by a Japanese village in the early 1900s. Our cosy nooks and large booths make it suitable for locals and visitors alike. Whether you're on a romantic first date or looking to celebrate a milestone birthday, we've got a spot for you!

Address

205-4000 Whistler way

What I like about it

My favourite thing to eat here is the honey garlic chicken, I have it almost every time! The food is made fresh and you don't need to wait a long time after ordering to eat. Staff is super friendly, and the food is really really good!

Creek Bread

Creekbread at Creekside is our first British Columbia effort. What a setting we have to bake flatbreads… in the heart of the Canadian Coast Mountains! Our crew is energized by the surrounding and when you come in, we want to make you happy. The Whistler-Creekside community has shown us tremendous gracious support and it is our job to return it with interest.

Address

2021 Karen Crescent

What I like about it

The pizza here is to DIE for! They have different specials be it meat or veggie. The specials change every season. The maple habanero sauce is a must try! They even sell it by the bottle! Great place for families as well.
